About Me

The person behind the work

I'm Arnold — a technology professional with over three decades of hands-on experience spanning systems development, Oracle database administration, IT leadership, and digital marketing operations. My career started at the keyboard in 1988 and has never really left it.

"I bridge the gap between technical systems and business outcomes — and now I'm applying that same mindset to building digital products and online brands."

After years leading IT departments and managing large virtual assistant teams, I've pivoted toward the creator economy: writing and self-publishing eBooks, producing faceless content for social platforms, building affiliate marketing sites, and offering creative services in copywriting, graphic design, and video editing.

Based in Davao City, Philippines — working globally, asynchronously, and independently.
